 How It Works

We are still working on our membership model. In the interim we are using a pay-as-you-go system:

  1. Create an account or login to your existing account.
  2. Browse the sessions available and click "Book" next to the ones you want to attend.
  3. Bring a method of payment with to you the class (see below).

Payment is taken at the end of the session by credit or debit card. This includes phones with Apple Pay or Google Pay enabled, contactless cards and chip-and-pin cards. The trainer's fee is £76.34, which is split evenly between all the attending members, but capped at £8. For example: if fifteen people attend then the fee per person is £5.09. If twenty people attend then the fee is only £3.82 each! However, if only eight people attend then the fee is the maximum £8.

 Trainers
  • Angela Charalambous

    Angela is a fully qualified L2 & L3 Fitness Instructor and Personal Trainer and Hatton Academy Certified Boxing for Fitness instructor. She is passionate, enthusiastic, disciplined and strives to get the best out of everyone no matter their ability. As a busy mum of three who also works for the family business, Angela rediscovered her love for sport and exercise back in 2011 under the guidance of her late friend and mentor Andy Jones.

    Angela’s classes are challenging but rewarding, she will keep you motivated and encourage you to keep pushing and believing in yourself. There is no room in her vocabulary for the words “I can’t” only “I’ll try”!

  • Javier Suarez

    Javier is a total enthusiast about fitness and human movement. Currently serving as a firefighter with the London Fire Brigade, Javier always manages to squeeze in time to share his passion for training with others. With years of experience, Javier has worked for several fitness providers such as British Military Fitness where was in charge of the whole West London operation.

    His qualifications include: L4 Strength and Conditioning coach; L3 Personal Trainer; L2 Fitness Instructor; Senior Kettlebells trainer.

  • Andy Jones

    Andy was the founder of PowerFit Outdoors, a former lead instructor with British Military Fitness, a mountain expedition leader, and a great friend and mentor to many members of our community.

    Andy passed away in 2024 and is very deeply missed by us all.

 Locations
  • Oak Hill Park

    Oak Hill Park is at Parkside Gardens, London EN4 8JP in Barnet Borough. Our meeting point is at the outdoor tables next to the car park.

  • Trent Park

    Trent Park is accessible from Cockfosters Road, London EN4 0PS in Enfield Borough. Our meeting point is at the “Duke's Pyramid” near to Go Ape.

    Ample car parking is available. Please note that car access to the meeting point is only possible from Cockfosters Road, not any of the other park entrances.
