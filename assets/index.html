 How It Works

We are still working on our membership model. In the interim, we are using a pay-as-you-go system:

  1. Create an account or login to your existing account.
  2. Browse the sessions available and click "Book" next to the ones you want to attend.
  3. Bring a method of payment with to you the class (see below).

Payment is taken at the end of the session by credit or debit card. This includes phones with Apple Pay or Google Pay enabled, contactless cards and chip-and-pin cards.  Locations
  • Oak Hill Park

    Oak Hill Park is at Parkside Gardens, London EN4 8JP in Barnet Borough. Our meeting point is at the outdoor tables next to the car park.

  • Trent Park

    Trent Park is accessible from Cockfosters Road, London EN4 0PS in Enfield Borough. Our meeting point is at the “Duke's Pyramid” near to Go Ape.

    Ample car parking is available. Please note that car access to the meeting point is only possible from Cockfosters Road, not any of the other park entrances.
